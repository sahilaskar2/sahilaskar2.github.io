About Me

I am a Computer Science Engineering student with a strong interest in the field of cybersecurity. My primary goal is to understand how modern digital systems operate and how they can be protected from evolving cyber threats. I am particularly interested in areas such as network security, web security, and ethical hacking.

Through my academic curriculum and self-learning, I am gaining knowledge of core cybersecurity concepts including threat analysis, vulnerability assessment, secure system design, and basic penetration testing methodologies. I actively explore how security principles are applied in real-world scenarios, especially in web applications and networked environments.

Along with cybersecurity, I have an interest in web development, which helps me better understand application-level security risks and defenses. By combining both domains, I aim to design and analyze systems that are not only functional but also secure and reliable.

This website represents my learning journey, projects, and practical exposure in cybersecurity and related technologies. It also reflects my continuous effort to improve my technical skills, stay updated with current security practices, and build a strong foundation for a future career in cybersecurity.
